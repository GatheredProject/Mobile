<h1 align="center">Gathered Mobile</h1>

Gathered Mobile is a powerful mobile app designed to bring church communities closer together through prayer and praise. With Gathered, users can easily share prayer requests and praise reports, creating a space for others to lift them up in prayer or join in celebrating God's faithfulness. 

Whether you're seeking support or giving thanks, Gathered makes it simple to connect, encourage, and strengthen your faith alongside others.

## Features

### Submit Prayer Requests & Praise Reports
Share your prayer requests and praise reports with the community—either openly or anonymously—empowering others to join in prayer or celebrate your victories.

### Community Feed
Stay connected with the latest prayer requests and praise reports from others in the app. The community feed keeps you updated and inspired by the stories and prayers being shared.

### Smart Filters
Easily find what you're looking for with our intuitive filtering options! Sort posts by date, category, or status to find specific requests or reports that resonate with you.

### Daily Curated Highlights
Every day, discover a handpicked collection of prayer requests and praise reports, carefully chosen to inspire and encourage. Stay focused on what matters most, each day.

### Create & Organize Groups
Build meaningful connections by creating and managing your own prayer or support groups. Organize your community, track prayer progress, and offer mutual encouragement.

### Prayer Reminders & Notifications
Stay in the loop with real-time notifications for when a prayer request is answered or when an urgent prayer need is posted. Never miss an opportunity to celebrate a victory or step in to support someone in immediate need.
